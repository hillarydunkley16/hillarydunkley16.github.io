About Me:

I am a student at Smith College in Massachusetts, double majoring in Computer Science and Music. When I attended my first hackathon at 15 years old I became fascinated by coding. I enjoy collaborating with others to complete a project and expressing my creativity through technology. In March 2020, I created a mutual aid website in collaboration with my older sister, helping vulnerable neighbors in my community. As a violinist and a singer, I am especially interested in the intersection of technology and the arts. I started my own business in my freshman year of high school teaching violin, viola and singing lessons to adults and children. During the past two years, this business has shifted entirely online. Adapting and adjusting using new technology while continuing to meet my studentsâ€™ needs has been both challenging and rewarding. My hobbies are composing and performing music. In my free time I enjoy discovering new music artists, reading classic literature and binge watching my guilty pleasure shows.
